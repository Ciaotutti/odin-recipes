Broccoli and Stilton Soup

Description

A smooth, blended vegetable soup with blue cheese that's as good for a comforting meal as it is for a dinner party starter

Ingredients

  • 1tbsp rapeseed oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1stick celery, sliced
  • 1 leek, sliced
  • 1 medium potato, diced
  • 1 knob butter
  • 1l low salt or homemade chicken or vegetable stock
  • 1 head broccoli, roughly chopped
  • Stilton, or other blue cheese, crumbled

Method

  1. STEP 1 Heat 2 tbsp rapeseed oil in a large saucepan and then add 1 finely chopped onion. Cook on a medium heat until soft. Add a splash of water if the onion starts to catch.

  2. STEP 2 Add 1 sliced celery stick, 1 sliced leek, 1 diced medium potato and a knob of butter. Stir until melted, then cover with a lid. Allow to sweat for 5 minutes then remove the lid.

  3. STEP 3 Pour in 1l of chicken or vegetable stock and add any chunky bits of stalk from 1 head of broccoli. Cook for 10-15 minutes until all the vegetables are soft.

  4. STEP 4 Add the rest of the roughly chopped broccoli and cook for a further 5 minutes

  5. STEP 5 Carefully transfer to a blender and blitz until smooth.

  6. STEP 6 Stir in 140g crumbled stilton, allowing a few lumps to remain. Season with black pepper and serve
